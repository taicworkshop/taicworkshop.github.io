Acknowledgment

This workshop has been organized in the frame of the following European Projects (HORIZON EUROPE):
Sec4AI4Sec (Grant Agreement No 101120393)
CoEvolution (Grant Agreement No 101168560)

The Sec4AI4Sec project aims to create a range of cutting-edge technologies, open-source tools, and new methodologies for designing and certifying secure AI-enhanced systems and AI-enhanced systems for security.
The CoEvolution project's main objective instead is to focus on establishing a comprehensive security and risk assessment process for AI models throughout their lifecycle, ensuring security-by-design under various paradigms (single, collaborative, or interconnected AI agents).
